Services.

We are a company that provides babysitting, pet sitting, and tutoring services in addition to selling treats!

Our babysitting service is for potty trained kids up to age 12. Rates for babysitting are $10-$20 / hour, varying depending on the number and age of children.

Our pet sitting service is mainly for cats and dogs, but most other household pets are available for pet sitting too. Feel free to contact us if you are not sure if your pet qualifies for pet sitting! Rates for pet sitting are $15-$20 / day, varying depending on the number and type of animals.

Our tutoring service can be ordered the same day as the appointment! The service includes the subjects: Math, History, English / Language Arts, and Spanish*.

For our tutoring service, a parent/guardian must be home during the appointment, unless scheduled with babysitting.

  • All tutoring excluding Spanish can be ordered the same day of the appointment
  • All textbooks, books, and schoolwork must be provided
  • Special requests can be made for other subjects, but some are not available

    • *Spanish Tutoring must be ordered ahead of time

About Us.

Our names are Aidan and Emily! We are two hard-working high school students at OPRF high school who decided to start a babysitting business that benefited the environment and our town. We are both CPR certified and have taken a Safe Sitting class with the Park District of Oak Park.

10% of our profits go towards charities (The ASPCA and The Climate Reality Project). We are qualified to tutor for students up to 8th grade for Math, History, Language Arts / English, and Spanish.
